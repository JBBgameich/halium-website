Introduction

Halium is a project that aims to unify the hardware adaption layers (HAL) of different projects targetting mobile android devices. For end-users, Halium will just be a part of their phone system, that enables using sensors, camera and hardware-accelerated graphaics. Technically, a Halium base consists of libhybris, an android kernel, and an android rootfs or image supplying binary drivers.

The project is still in an early stage, but you can follow our project at Github.
